Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 10, 2025

This privacy policy applies to the Font Quiz app for iOS, developed as an independent app. This page explains what information is collected and how it is used.

Information Collected

Font Quiz does not collect, store, or transmit any personal information directly. The app does not require you to create an account or provide any personal details to use it.

Advertising

Font Quiz displays advertisements provided by Google AdMob. These ads are non-personalised and are not based on your personal interests or browsing history.

Google AdMob may collect certain technical data to serve ads, such as your device type, operating system, and general location (country/region). This data is handled in accordance with Google's own privacy policy: https://policies.google.com/privacy

For more information on how Google uses data from apps that use its services, please visit: https://policies.google.com/technologies/partner-sites

Changes to This Policy

This privacy policy may be updated from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated date. We encourage you to review this page periodically.
